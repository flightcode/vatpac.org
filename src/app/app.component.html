Welcome

Welcome to VATPAC, the home of VATSIM Australia Pacific. VATPAC coordinates simulated flying and manages air traffic control services in over 10% of the world's airspace, including all Australian airspace as well as various South Pacific regions and Oceanic sectors. VATSIM is a great place to practice your flying skills and enhance your experience whether you are a flight simulator enthusiast or a real-world pilot. VATSIM is a non-profit community of over 400,000 members from across the world who have a passion for all things aviation, and who strive to provide a realistic online flight-simulation network. We look forward to seeing you online soon!
